At NixAi, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use Cyanix AI. By using Cyanix AI, you agree to the practices described in this document.

01

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Cyanix AI:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and authentication credentials when you register for an account
  • Conversation Data: Messages you send to and receive from Cyanix AI, stored to enable persistent chat history across sessions
  • Uploaded Files: Files you choose to attach to conversations, including images, documents, and code, stored securely in our cloud infrastructure
  • Usage Data: Interaction logs, feature usage patterns, session durations, and error reports used to improve the service
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, device information, operating system, and session tokens used for security and platform functionality
  • Profile Preferences: Display name, avatar, tone and interface settings that you configure within the platform

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, government identification numbers, or health data.

02

How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to operate, maintain, and improve Cyanix AI. Specifically, your data is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Cyanix AI platform and its features
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account against unauthorised access
  • Store and retrieve your chat history and preferences across sessions
  • Improve AI model performance and platform safety using anonymised and aggregated data
  • Send service-related communications, including security alerts and policy updates
  • Monitor and analyse usage trends to improve user experience and platform stability
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities
Our CommitmentWe do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve Cyanix AI.
03

Cylink Integration

Cylink is a core, built-in feature of Cyanix AI that retrieves real-time information from external platforms to enhance AI responses. Cylink currently integrates with the following services:

  • DuckDuckGo: Used for live web search. Queries are processed through DuckDuckGo's API, which has its own privacy-preserving policies and does not track individual users
  • YouTube Data API: Used to retrieve relevant video results. Queries sent to YouTube are subject to Google's Privacy Policy

When you send a message to Cyanix AI, your query may be forwarded to these external services to gather contextual information. We do not transmit your account details, personal profile, or chat history to these third-party services. Search queries are processed solely to generate contextual AI responses and are not stored beyond the duration of your session.

04

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a secure open-source backend platform built on PostgreSQL. Supabase provides encrypted database storage, file storage, and authentication infrastructure hosted on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure.

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption of data at rest within Supabase's managed infrastructure
  • Role-based access controls ensuring only authorised systems can access your data
  • Secure authentication flows using industry-standard OAuth 2.0 providers
  • Regular security reviews and infrastructure monitoring

While we implement stringent security measures, no system is entirely free from risk. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to enable two-factor authentication wherever available.

05

Data Retention

We retain your conversation data, account information, and uploaded files for as long as your account remains active. You may delete individual conversations directly from within the platform at any time.

If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, you may do so from your profile settings or by contacting us at nixai.cy@gmail.com. Upon receiving a verified deletion request:

  • Your data will be permanently removed from all active systems within 30 days
  • Anonymised, aggregated data derived from your usage may be retained for platform improvement purposes
  • Backup retention of encrypted snapshots may extend up to 90 days before permanent deletion
06

Third-Party Services

Cyanix AI integrates with the following third-party authentication providers to enable account sign-in:

  • Google
  • Microsoft
  • GitHub
  • Discord

When you use OAuth sign-in via any of these providers, we receive only the information that provider shares with us according to your authorisation — typically your name, email address, and profile picture. We do not receive your passwords or payment information from these providers.

We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party providers. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies before linking your accounts.

08

Cookies

Cyanix AI uses only essential cookies required for the platform to function correctly. These include:

  • Session Cookies: Used to maintain your authenticated session while you are using the platform
  • Preference Cookies: Used to remember your interface settings such as theme and language preferences

We do not use advertising cookies, behavioural tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, doing so may impact the functionality of the platform, including your ability to remain signed in.

Your Rights

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data
